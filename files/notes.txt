# 📘 Basic Math Notes

## 🔹 What is Mathematics?
Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns. It is used in science, engineering, economics, and daily life.

---

## 🔹 Number Systems

### 1. Natural Numbers
1, 2, 3, 4, ...

### 2. Whole Numbers
0, 1, 2, 3, ...

### 3. Integers
..., -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, ...

### 4. Rational Numbers
Numbers that can be written as a fraction (e.g., 1/2, 3/4)

### 5. Irrational Numbers
Cannot be written as fractions (e.g., √2, π)

---

## 🔹 Basic Operations

### Addition (+)
Example: 2 + 3 = 5

### Subtraction (-)
Example: 5 - 2 = 3

### Multiplication (×)
Example: 4 × 3 = 12

### Division (÷)
Example: 10 ÷ 2 = 5

---

## 🔹 Algebra

### Variables
Letters used to represent numbers (e.g., x, y)

### Expression
Combination of numbers and variables  
Example: 2x + 3

### Equation
Two expressions are equal  
Example: 2x + 3 = 7

---

## 🔹 Geometry

### Common Shapes
- Triangle
- Square
- Circle
- Rectangle

### Area Formulas
- Square: side × side
- Rectangle: length × width
- Triangle: 1/2 × base × height
- Circle: πr²

---

## 🔹 Trigonometry

### Basic Ratios
- sinθ = opposite / hypotenuse
- cosθ = adjacent / hypotenuse
- tanθ = opposite / adjacent

---

## 🔹 Calculus

### Derivative
Rate of change  
Example: d/dx (x²) = 2x

### Integration
Area under a curve  
Example: ∫x dx = x²/2

---

## 🔹 Probability

Probability = (favorable outcomes) / (total outcomes)

Example:
Chance of getting head in a coin toss = 1/2

---

## 🔹 Statistics

### Mean
Average = sum of values / number of values

### Median
Middle value

### Mode
Most frequent value

---

## 🔹 Important Formulas

- (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²  
- (a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b²  
- a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

---

## 🔹 Tips

- Practice daily
- Understand concepts, don’t memorize blindly
- Solve problems step by step
- Revise formulas regularly

---

## ✅ Summary

Mathematics includes numbers, algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics.  
It helps solve real-world problems and builds logical thinking.
